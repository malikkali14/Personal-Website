The Future of Chelsea

As a student I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor of Commerce at the University of Ottawa. While taking different classes, I was able to discover a burgeoning passion for the world of software and coding. That’s why you’re on this website right now! I’ve always been a curious person, constantly falling down different rabbit-holes (just ask my friends). So I thought, why not use my passion for tech to help express and showcase all those different interests. Some of the things I’d love to talk about are my favourite sports teams (Raptors and Chelsea mainly), some of my favourite movies, things in the news that interest me, and anything else I find cool! Asides from that, I’d also love to show you the different coding projects I’m undertaking. Finally, thank you for stopping by on my little corner of the internet. My hope is that others who come here can get a better sense of who I am through my projects and discussions. ~M.A.
